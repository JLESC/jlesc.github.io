4th JLESC Summer School

Summer School Profile
  • 20 Jul 2017
  • 21 Jul 2017
  • NCSA Building, Urbana, Illinois, USA
  • National Center for Supercomputing Applications
  • Event's Main Page

The fourth JLESC Summer School will be held at NCSA from July 20 to 21, 2017, following the 7th JLESC Worksphop.

The summer school will feature two days of talks on topics like Python, MPI, OpenACC and more. The school is offered to students (mainly PhD students and post-docs) of the collaboration and open to students and faculties outside of the collaboration, if room.

See here for more information. The program is now also available!
