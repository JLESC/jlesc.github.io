3rd JLESC Summer School

Summer School Profile
  • 30 Jun 2016
  • 01 Jul 2016
  • ENS, Lyon, France
  • Institut national de recherche en informatique et en automatique
  • Event's Main Page
  • Schedule

The third JLESC Summer School will be held at ENS-Lyon, from June 30 to July 1, 2016, following the 5th JLESC Worksphop.

The topic of this summer school is resilience and it is jointly organized by Franck Cappello (ANL), Aurélien Cavelan (INRIA), Leonard Bautista Gomez (BSC) and Yves Robert (INRIA). The school is offered to students (mainly PhD students and post-docs) of the collaboration and open to students and faculties outside of the collaboration, if room. Up to a maximum of 24 students are considered.

The summer school is supported by the PUF in the context of the NEXTGN project.

Registration will take place at http://jlesc2016.sciencesconf.org.
